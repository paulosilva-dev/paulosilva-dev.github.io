Bolo Ministro

Ingredients

Cake

  • 150 ml vegetable oil
  • 200 ml milk
  • 300 g sugar
  • 300 g self-rising flour
  • 60 g cinnamon
  • 80 g chocolate powder

Syrup

  • 150 g sugar
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 300 ml water

Filling

  • 1 lemon peel
  • 1 l milk
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 200 g sugar
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 80 corbflour
  • cinnamon (for sprinkling)

Instructions

For the Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 º C.
  2. Whisk the sugar and the eggs
  3. Add the remaining ingredients mix them well.
  4. Bake in a greased round shape for about 45 minutes.
  5. Let the cake cool, trim it all around and cut it in half, separating the halves with the inside up.

For the Syrup

  1. Bring all the ingredients to a boil and simmer about 3 minutes, until the syrup flows into wide strips when you dip a spoon and lift it.
  2. Use a fork to create some holes on the two halves of the cake.
  3. Spread the Syrup over the two halves.

For the Filling

  1. Mix all the ingredients except the lemon peel and cinnamon stick.
  2. Add the lemon and cinnamon to the mix and let it boil at a moderate temperature.
  3. Remove the lemon peel and cinnamon stick, pour the cream into a large dish and let it cool.
  4. Spread it in the base and top of the cake.
  5. Put the two halves back together and spread the rest of the filling on top and around the cake.
  6. Sprinkle with the cinnamon powder

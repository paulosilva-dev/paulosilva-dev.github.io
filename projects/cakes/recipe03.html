Pudim caseiro

Ingredients

  • 15 eggs
  • 15 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 l milk
  • Liquid caramel

Instructions

  1. Spread caramel in the baking pan with. .
  2. Mix all the ingredients together.
  3. Put the mix in the baking Pan
  4. Put them in the oven in a water bath for 1h30m
  5. Let it cool before unmolding.

Pasteis de nata

Ingredients

  • 600 g Puff Pastry
  • 500 ml milk
  • 1 lemon peel
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 60 g wheat flour
  • 500g Sugar
  • 250 ml water
  • 7 gems

Instructions

  1. With the rolling pin, stretch the dough into a rectangle.
  2. Cut the dough into circles with a thickness of 1.5 cm
  3. Place them in a greased muffin Baking Pan
  4. Dissolve the flour in a little milk.
  5. Bring the remaining milk to the boil, add the cinnamon stick and lemon peel.
  6. When it starts boiling, add the flour.
  7. Stir until it starts to boil and turn off the heat.
  8. In a pan, let it boil sugar and water and stir . Once you start to boil, simmer exactly 3 minutes.
  9. Add the sugar syrup to the milk and mix well.
  10. Add the egg yolks with the cream and stir well.
  11. Fill the forms with the cream.
  12. Put them in a preheated oven at 250º for 17 minutes.
  13. After cooked, unmold. Serve hot or cold, sprinkled with powdered sugar or cinnamon.
